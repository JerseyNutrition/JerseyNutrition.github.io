What is Nutrition Professionals Jersey?

Nutrition Professionals Jersey is a collective of qualified nutrition professionals that are members of a recognised accrediting body.

Our Purpose

  1. Provide the people of Jersey with assurance about the quality, safety, and effectiveness of their nutrition practitioner
  2. Educate the people of Jersey on the distinguishing factors between different nutrition practitioners
  3. Promote registered and qualified practitioners
